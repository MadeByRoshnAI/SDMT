My Mukhpath Mandal

See how fellow haribhaktas are progressing on their mukhpath journey. If you want your name to appear on this page for your friends/karyakars to see make sure your name is set in 'My Profile' and add in your favourite shlok that you've learnt so far!

Remember we're all doing this mukhpath together with the same goal so lets use this page as a way to encourage and motivate each other to keep learning our shloks!

Quick Guide
🏠 Home
🙏
My Mukhpath Journey
Track your progress toward all 315 shloks with a progress bar and milestone badges.
📖
Daily Cards
Shlok of the Day, your Daily Challenge Streak, 'Revise Now' and your Next Milestone - all at a glance.
📈
Progress Breakdown
A chart showing how many shloks are learnt, memorised, revise or not started.
✏️
Edit Profile
Tap 'My Profile' on the right edge to set your name, centre, start date and favourite shlok.
🎯
Next Milestone
Open 'My Profile' on the right edge to see your next milestone and how many shloks away you are - plus your learnt, memorised and revise counts.
🔒
Keep Your Progress Safe
Sign in with Google on the home page to back up your progress. Sign in again anytime to restore everything. Sign out from 'My Profile'.
📘 My Mukhpath
🗺️
Shlok Map
A visual grid of all 315 shloks, colour-coded by status - see your mukhpath overview.
➕
Add Shloks
Add a shlok one at a time, or use 'Add Range' to add a batch of shloks in one go.
🏆
Track Status
Mark each shlok as learnt, memorised or revise. Use 'Update Status' to bulk change a range, or 'Remove Shlok' if you accidentally added one.
📚 Learn
📖
Understand First
Before practising, read the full shlok and its meaning - understanding what it means makes it much easier to remember.
🧠
Line by Line
Learn line 1, then line 2, then say the whole shlok together - learning the shlok step by step before testing your memory.
✅
Recall & Add
Recall the whole shlok from just a two-word clue. Once you can do it, add it as learnt and move on to the next shlok.
📝
Revise Tab
Switch to the Revise tab to study shloks marked as revise. Tricky parts are blurred so you can try to recall them first - tap to reveal. Head to Revision Mode when you're ready to test yourself.

🙏
Quotes
Tap 'New Quote' for some inspiration and motivation for your mukhpath journey, then tap the heart to save favourites.

Videos

⏳
Paris Countdown
A live countdown to Paris Mandir Mahotsav - see how many days you have left and use it as motivation to keep learning!

Revision Mode
Test your revise shloks one by one. If you get one wrong, tag which part you couldn't remember - line 1, line 2, a specific word or the whole shlok. Next time it appears, a hint will remind you where to focus.
